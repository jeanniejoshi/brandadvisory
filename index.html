Global Brand Strategy & Innovation Leader | Creative Executive | Generative Design in Branding & AI Governance | Design‑Tech Educator | Strategic Partnerships & Culture

Senior Advisor and Head of Brand specializing in brand innovation across consumer goods and healthcare. Advises leadership teams on brand strategy, portfolio positioning, and growth. Expertise includes corporate marketing in Corporate Affairs at Bristol Myers Squibb, design management at Bath & Body Works, and founding a strategic design practice serving clients including L'Oréal, Godiva, and Hasbro. Methodology integrates cultural analysis, semiotics, and applied ethnography into innovation and strategy. Writes and speaks on brand strategy, AI governance, design futures, and generative AI in creative practice. Has taught as a Visiting Assistant Professor at Pratt Institute and Adjunct Lecturer at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, and is a returning judge for the BeautyMatter NEXT Awards.

Harmonizing the analytic and creative, seeking solutions that proclaim vitality over time for the established brand and achieve maximum initial impact for a new brand.
